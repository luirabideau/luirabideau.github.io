## What are WODs?
WODs, or Workouts Of the Day, are take-home assignments meant to help students become more familiar with the class material. 
My Approach
I would do the readings and other material prior to class, but for the WODs, I did not do any preparation. This was a conscious choice on my part, because I want to gauge how much of the material I retained. 
A few of my first attempts at the WODs were successful and I managed to get Rx well within the time. 
I found that blindly trying the WOD, and then watching the recommended video if the first attempt was unsuccessful was the best use of my time. I did not change my approach to WODs, mostly because I did not encounter any issues with them. 
What I Recommend
As long as you understand the general concept of what the WOD is about, I think trying it blindly is the best first attempt method. If that doesn’t work or if you don’t understand what is going on, I recommend googling, reading, watching the video, and/or asking the professor about it. If all else fails; OFFICE HOURS.
