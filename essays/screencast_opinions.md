## Overview
I think the screencast lectures are decently beneficial. If you’re completely lost or stuck on the WODs, they are really a saving grace. Are they the best? No, far from it. Some screencasts give the answers but don’t really explain what exactly is happening. 
## Purpose
I assume the primarily purpose of the screencasts is to help students understand what is going on, and the secondary purpose is to give the answers. 
## Suggestions
It would be nice to see more detailed explanations of the concepts in the videos. I would want a narrated, edited section of someone doing the WOD, accompanied by a second section of the video explaining how the WOD works on a more detailed level then currently. The videos could be edited to have timestamps. Additionally, cutting out the boring or silent bits would improve the video.
