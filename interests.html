Early Interests

When I was 15 years old, I purchased Minecraft on my phone for the first time. Since then, I have not looked back. Minecraft is a 3D sandbox game (sandbox means it’s up to the player to decide their own gaming experience).

It is a playground to build anything you want in a highly customizable virtual reality. Furthermore, it is a multiplayer game. Meaning you can play with anyone in the world as long as you connect to the same server.

Projects

Broadway–Cortlandt Building

An interpretist work of New York's late Broadway-Cortlandt Building.

I have been wanting to do a build in the Romanesque Revival architectural style for years but never felt particularly inspired. One glance at the original Broadway-Cortlandt Building had me sold. Built in less than 5 hours with the use of Fabric's World Edit. Rendered with BSL Shaders on Optifine.
Rush Rhees Library

A repulica of the University of Rochester's Rush Rhees Library.

This beautiful building takes inspiration from multiple architectural styles including Greek Revival, Georgian, and Beaux-Arts (which incorporates Renaissance and Baroque elements). Although it is not Built with the use of Fabric's World Edit. Rendered with BSL Shaders on Optifine.
